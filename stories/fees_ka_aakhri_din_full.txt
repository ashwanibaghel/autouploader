TITLE: Fees Ka Aakhri Din

MOOD: Student_Struggle

Us din college ki fees bharne ka aakhri din tha.

Class ke sab bachche apni padhai mein lage hue the...

Aur main...

Apne phone ki screen par account balance dekh raha tha.

₹327.

Bas itne hi paise the.

Fees thi ₹18,500.

Teacher padhate rahe...

Lekin mera dhyaan sirf ek baat par tha.

"Kal se college aana band karna padega kya?"

Maine kisi ko kuch nahi bataya.

Na doston ko.

Na ghar par.

Shaam ko ghar pahucha.

Maa ne poocha,

"Beta padhai kaisi chal rahi hai?"

Main muskuraya aur bola,

"Sab badhiya hai."

Sach to ye tha...

Kuch bhi badhiya nahi tha.

Us raat neend nahi aayi.

Main chhat par baitha raha.

Sochta raha ki itni mehnat karne ke baad bhi...

Shayad sapne paise se hi jeette hain.

Subah college jaane ki himmat nahi hui.

Main ghar ke bahar hi baitha tha...

Tab papa ka phone aaya.

Unhone sirf itna poocha,

"Beta, fees bhar di?"

Mere muh se awaaz hi nahi nikli.

Kuch der chup rehne ke baad maine kaha,

"Nahi papa..."

Phone ke doosri taraf bhi khamoshi thi.

Mujhe laga...

Ab daant padegi.

Lekin papa ne bas itna kaha,

"Chinta mat kar."

Aur phone kaat diya.

Main samajh nahi paya.

Do ghante baad papa ghar aaye.

Unke haath mein paise the.

Aur kalai khaali.

Maine poocha,

"Papa ghadi kahan hai?"

Papa muskura diye.

Aur bole,

"Purani ho gayi thi."

Us din pehli baar mujhe samajh aaya...

Kuch log waqt dekhne ke liye ghadi pehente hain.

Aur kuch log...

Apne bachchon ka bhavishya bachane ke liye ghadi bech dete hain.

Us raat main bahut roya.

Lekin kisi dard ki wajah se nahi.

Ek zimmedari ki wajah se.

Tab maine khud se ek wada kiya.

Ek din aisa zaroor aayega...

Jab papa ko kisi cheez ki keemat dekhkar faisla nahi lena padega.

Aur shayad...

Meri asli padhai usi din shuru hui thi.
