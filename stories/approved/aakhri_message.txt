TITLE: Aakhri Message

MOOD: Friendship

Ek dost tha...

Jisse main roz baat karta tha.

Din kaisa gaya,

Kya hua,

Sab kuch.

Phir dheere-dheere zindagi busy ho gayi.

Uske messages aate the...

Aur main sochta tha,

"Baad me reply kar dunga."

Ek din uska message aaya,

"Bhai, baat karni thi."

Maine phir socha...

"Kal kar lunga."

Lekin kal kabhi nahi aaya.

Agli subah pata chala...

Woh hospital me tha.

Aur uske baad...

Uska koi message nahi aaya.

Aaj bhi chat kholta hun,

To uska woh aakhri message dikhta hai:

"Bhai, baat karni thi."

Tab samajh aaya...

Har message notification nahi hota.

Kuch log...

Madad maang rahe hote hain.

Aur zindagi ki sabse buri feeling ye nahi hai ki koi chala gaya.

Sabse buri feeling ye hai...

Ki tum reply kar sakte the.

Par tumne kiya nahi.
