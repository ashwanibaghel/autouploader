TITLE: Sasta Pen

MOOD: Life_Lesson

College me sab expensive pens use karte the.

Mere paas ek sasta sa pen tha.

Mujhe sharm aati thi.

Ek din exam me mera pen kharab ho gaya.

Mere paas baitha ladka,

Jiske paas sabse mehenga pen tha,

Uska bhi pen ruk gaya.

Hum dono ne paas baithe ek ladke se pen maanga.

Usne muskura kar de diya.

Us din samajh aaya...

Keemat cheezon ki nahi hoti.

Keemat waqt par kaam aane ki hoti hai.

Insaan bhi waise hi hote hain.

Mehenga banna zaroori nahi.

Kaam ka banna zaroori hai.
