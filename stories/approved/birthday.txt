TITLE: Birthday

MOOD: Family_Emotion

Mera birthday tha.

Subah se ek hi umeed thi...

Ki papa wish karenge.

Lekin unka koi call nahi aaya.

Mujhe bura laga.

Shaam tak gussa bhi aa gaya.

Raat ko maa ka phone aaya.

Unhone bataya...

Papa subah 5 baje hi kaam par nikal gaye the.

Extra shift karne.

Taaki is mahine meri coaching fees bhar saken.

Us din samajh aaya...

Kuch log "Happy Birthday" nahi bol paate.

Kyuki woh chupchaap tumhari zindagi better banane me lage hote hain.

Har khamoshi laparwahi nahi hoti.
