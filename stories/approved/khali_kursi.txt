TITLE: Khali Kursi

MOOD: Family_Emotion

Roz shaam ko dadaji us kursi par baithte the.

Aur main unke paas.

Unki kahaniyan sunta tha.

Kabhi-kabhi bore bhi ho jata tha.

Phir ek din...

Woh kursi khali reh gayi.

Ab ghar me sab kuch waisa hi hai.

Bas kahaniyan sunane wala koi nahi.

Tab samajh aaya...

Hum aksar un logon ki kadar der se karte hain,

Jinke hone ki aadat ho jaati hai.

Jo aaj saath hain,

Unke liye waqt nikal lo.

Kal sirf yaadein bachti hain.
