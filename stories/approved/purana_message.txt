TITLE: Purana Message

MOOD: Love_Lesson

Ek din purani chats dekh raha tha.

Har jagah "Take Care" likha hua tha.

Us waqt lagta tha...

Ye bas ek normal sa message hai.

Aaj saalon baad samajh aaya...

Har koi "Take Care" nahi kehta.

Kuch log usme apni poori fikr chhupa dete hain.

Mohabbat hamesha "I Love You" nahi bolti.

Kabhi-kabhi woh sirf itna kehti hai...

"Ghar pahunch kar message kar dena."

Pyar ko bade lafzon me mat dhoondhna.

Woh chhoti chhoti baaton me chhupa hota hai.
