TITLE: Pehla Number

MOOD: Student_Life

Main class me first aaya tha.

Certificate lekar ghar bhaaga.

Socha sabse pehle papa ko dikhaunga.

Ghar pahucha...

To papa so rahe the.

Maa ne kaha,

"Raat bhar duty ki thi."

Main certificate lekar unke paas baith gaya.

Aur pehli baar notice kiya...

Unke baalon me safedi badh gayi thi.

Us din samajh aaya...

Meri report card me sirf mere number nahi the.

Usme papa ki neend bhi lagi thi.

Kamyabi kabhi akeli nahi aati.
