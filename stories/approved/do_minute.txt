TITLE: Do Minute

MOOD: Relationship_Lesson

Maa roz kehti thi,

"Bas do minute baith ja."

Aur main har baar kehta tha,

"Abhi time nahi hai."

Ek din job ke liye dusre shehar chala gaya.

Ab ghar aata hun...

To maa wahi kehti hain,

"Bas do minute baith ja."

Aur main turant baith jata hun.

Kyuki ab samajh aaya...

Zindagi me paise dobara kama sakte ho.

Mauke bhi dobara mil sakte hain.

Lekin maa ke saath baithne ke woh do minute...

Har baar nahi milte.

Jin logon ko tum kal ke liye chhod rahe ho,

Ho sakta hai woh aaj hi tumhara intezar kar rahe hon.
