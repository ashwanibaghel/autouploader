TITLE: Aakhri Bench

MOOD: Self_Confidence

School me main hamesha aakhri bench par baithta tha.

Teachers ko lagta tha...

Main kuch nahi kar paunga.

Dost bhi mazaak uda dete the.

Ek din maine papa se poocha,

"Sabko lagta hai main fail ho jaunga."

Papa muskura kar bole,

"Beta, ped ki unchai uski shuruaat se nahi,

Uski mehnat se naapte hain."

Saalon baad jab result aaya,

Main sabse aage tha.

Tab samajh aaya...

Log tumhari shuruaat dekhte hain.

Bhagwan tumhari manzil dekhte hain.

Kabhi kisi ki rai ko apni pehchaan mat bana lena.
