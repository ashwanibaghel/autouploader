TITLE: Aaj Nahi

MOOD: Life_Lesson

Maine socha...

Aaj nahi.

Kal se mehnat karunga.

Kal aaya...

Phir maine kaha,

Kal se.

Ye silsila mahino tak chalta raha.

Ek din calendar dekha...

Aur samajh aaya.

Main sapne nahi taal raha tha.

Main apni zindagi taal raha tha.

Sabse mehenga shabd "kal" hota hai.

Kyuki uski keemat aksar poori zindagi chukati hai.
