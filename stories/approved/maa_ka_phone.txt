TITLE: Maa Ka Phone

MOOD: Family_Emotion

Maa roz phone karti thi.

Aur main aksar kehta tha,

"Busy hun, baad me baat karta hun."

Ek din kaam kam tha.

Maine socha...

Aaj khud call karunga.

Phone milaya.

Maa ne uthate hi kaha,

"Sab theek hai na beta?"

Main hans pada.

Maine poocha,

"Aapne ye kyun poocha?"

Maa boli,

"Tu khud phone kare...

To maa dar hi jaati hai."

Us din samajh aaya...

Jin logon ko hum normal samajh lete hain.

Unke liye hum poori duniya hote hain.

Mohabbat ko kabhi aadat mat samajhna.
