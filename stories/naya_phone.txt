TITLE: Naya Phone

MOOD: Family_Emotion

School mein sabke paas naya phone tha.

Bas mere paas nahi.

Roz ghar aakar maa se kehta tha,

"Mujhe bhi naya phone chahiye."

Maa har baar muskurakar kehti,

"Agale mahine le denge."

Lekin mujhe lagta tha...

Bas bahane bana rahi hain.

Ek din gusse mein maine keh diya,

"Aap log mere liye kuch karte hi nahi."

Maa chup rahi.

Aur main gusse mein apne kamre mein chala gaya.

Us raat paani peene utha...

To dekha maa silai kar rahi thi.

Raat ke 2 baje the.

Aankhen laal thi.

Haath thak chuke the.

Lekin silai machine abhi bhi chal rahi thi.

Main chupchaap wapas kamre mein aa gaya.

Agli subah maa ne mujhe ek lifafa diya.

Usme phone ke paise the.

Aur ek parchi thi.

Us par likha tha:

"Beta, phone to aaj mil jayega...

Bas ek baat yaad rakhna.

Jab kabhi kamane lagna,

To kisi apne ki majboori ko

Uski mohabbat ki kami mat samajhna."

Us din phone to mil gaya...

Lekin usse zyada keemti ek baat mil gayi.

Aaj bhi jab maa ka phone aata hai,

Main kabhi busy nahi hota.

Kyuki mujhe yaad hai...

Ek waqt tha,

Jab meri har khwahish ke liye

Woh apni neend tak bech deti thi.
