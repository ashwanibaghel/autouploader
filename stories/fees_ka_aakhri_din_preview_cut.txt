TITLE: Fees Ka Aakhri Din

MOOD: Student_Struggle

Us din college ki fees bharne ka aakhri din tha.

Main phone ki screen par account balance dekh raha tha.

₹327.

Fees thi ₹18,500.

"Kal se college aana band karna padega kya?"

Maa ne poocha, "Beta padhai kaisi chal rahi hai?"

Main muskuraya aur bola, "Sab badhiya hai."

Sach to ye tha... kuch bhi badhiya nahi tha.

Subah papa ka phone aaya.

"Beta, fees bhar di?"

Mere muh se awaaz nahi nikli.

"Nahi papa..."

Mujhe laga ab daant padegi.

Papa ne bas kaha, "Chinta mat kar."

Do ghante baad papa ghar aaye.

Unke haath mein paise the.

Aur kalai khaali.

"Papa ghadi kahan hai?"

Papa muskura diye.

"Purani ho gayi thi."

Us din pehli baar samajh aaya...

Kuch log waqt dekhne ke liye ghadi pehente hain.

Aur kuch log...

Apne bachchon ka bhavishya bachane ke liye ghadi bech dete hain.

Tab maine khud se ek wada kiya.

Ek din papa ko kisi cheez ki keemat dekhkar faisla nahi lena padega.
